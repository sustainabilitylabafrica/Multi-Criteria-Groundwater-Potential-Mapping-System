🌍 Multi-Criteria Groundwater Potential Mapping System

A decision-support system for identifying groundwater potential areas in Zimbabwe.

The system combines:

  • BGS Africa Groundwater Atlas — regional hydrogeological context (CC BY-SA 4.0)
  • Boruta feature selection — choose the predictors that actually carry signal
  • Support Vector Machine — binary classifier (Low / High potential)
  • Total Confidence Score (TCS) — four-component score out of 10 capturing how much we trust the prediction
  • Land Use Pressure modifier — adjusts the prediction based on the surveyor's land-use checklist
  • BGS regional-baseline cross-check — automatic disagreement flag against published yield classes
  • Expert review queue — surfaces predictions that need human judgment before development decisions

Designed for real-world deployment, with full audit trail per prediction.
