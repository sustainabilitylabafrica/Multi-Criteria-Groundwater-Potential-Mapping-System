Important Notes

  • Zimbabwean scale only. The dataset is published at 1:5,000,000 — polygon boundaries are accurate to roughly the nearest kilometre, not metre-perfect. A spatial join on a single GPS point returns the regional aquifer character, not a site-specific assessment.
  • Topmost aquifer only. Deeper aquifers are not represented.
  • No supplementary layers. The dataset does not contain faults, lineaments, recharge zones, aquifer extents, or borehole points. Phase 6 spatial validation that depends on those layers is currently deferred.
  • Internet Connectivity. Future versions of application will be able to work offline from a given GPS location

For a site-level assessment, the system uses the BGS data as regional context and relies on the surveyor's biophysical observations (Phase 2 of the decision-making roadmap) for spatial discrimination at sub-polygon scale.
