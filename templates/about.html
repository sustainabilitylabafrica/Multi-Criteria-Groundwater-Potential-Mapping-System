ℹ️ About

This system was engineered for real-world groundwater decision support in Zimbabwe. It combines field observations, a trained machine-learning classifier, and an authoritative hydrogeological reference dataset.

What it does

  • Takes a GPS reading from the field
  • Auto-suggests the geological class from the BGS Africa Groundwater Atlas, with optional surveyor override
  • Runs an SVM classifier on the surveyor's six biophysical observations
  • Cross-checks the result against the BGS regional yield baseline
  • Adjusts the prediction for land-use pressure (mining, urban sprawl, beneficial dams, etc.)
  • Computes a Total Confidence Score and routes uncertain predictions to a human-in-the-loop review queue
  • Produces a printable site-survey report and a date-range history report
  • Exports all surveys as GeoJSON for QGIS / ArcGIS analysis
